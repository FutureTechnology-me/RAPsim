\subsection{Detailed Description}
Provides most methods for editing the Grid\-Model.

Initializes the \doxyref{Grid\-Map\-Listener}{p.}{classsgs_1_1controller_1_1_grid_map_listener}. Loads images for the J\-G\-M (load\-Images\-New) Adds and deletes Grid\-Objects. Provides logics for neighbor detection and power-\/line drawing.
